Our Story

Obsession Brewed
Into Perfection

NOIR BREW began as a whisper in a dimly lit roastery in 2008. A single obsession: to find the world's most extraordinary coffee and share it without compromise.

We travel to the volcanic highlands of Ethiopia, the misty terraces of Yunnan, and the ancient farms of Yemen — sourcing beans that tell a story. Then we roast them with the precision of a craftsman and the passion of a poet.

Single Origin

Direct trade from 12 countries. Nothing blended. Everything intentional.

Dark Roast Mastery

Our signature roast profile, refined over 10,000 hours.

Zero Compromise

If it doesn't move us, it doesn't make the menu.
